Click on **Filter → Process Monitor filter** in Procmon.  

Click on **Tool → Process Tree** to view live processes.  

Open **Tcpview tool** to capture TCP/UDP packets.  

Right-click on any file and select **Who is** to get details.  

Open **RAMMap tool** to monitor the hard disk.  

Open **VMMap tool** to monitor virtual memory.  

Open **Cacheset tool** to monitor cache memory.
